Introduction to Kaash :

Kaash was conceptualised to create spaces where people feel good. True to our name, we believe in bringing spaces to life and make them shine by creating an environment, which support required function and contribute towards health and wellbeing of its users. Whether it's a public environment, heritage building, or a private space, our philosophy is to combine modern day technology with human centric light planning for a sustainable planet.

Light defines spatial experience, as it falls and reflects. As design consultants, Kaash - light engineers aims to elevate this experience with sustainable and human-centric light planning.

KAASH - LIGHT ENGINEERS

A professional lighting consultancy for all things light and bright. Our human-centric lighting designs reflect our commitment to functionality as well as sustainability and are thoughtfully executed to help you create your dream space. From heritage buildings to sports arenas, from public parks to your private zones, we aim to combine modern technology with energy efficient lighting schemes in the best interest of our clients as well as the planet.
